Privacy Notice

Information pursuant to Art. 13 GDPR

on the processing of personal data through the Helmholtz Zentrum München (HMGU) when using the app “ArtsyML”

 

 

1. Scope of data processing

In the following, we want to inform you about the scope of data collection, storage and use (hereinafter: "data processing", used in the sense of Art. 4 No. 2 GDPR) of your personal data by Helmholtz Zentrum München Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Umwelt und Gesundheit (GmbH) (hereinafter: "HMGU") when using our app "ArtsyML”. 

 

ArtsyML is a deep learning tool that blends any people in the real time video stream with a biological image. By redirecting to the ArtsyML page a camera captures your video. A video or an image of you is not saved unless by your request for taking a snapshot. In that case the actual image of you and its blended one are saved temporarily and automatically deleted after 1 minute.

 

2. Purposes of data processing 

Your personal data is processed solely to offer you the services of our app "ArtsyML”.

 

3. Categories of data processing         

When using our app "ArtsyML” a video, two pictures of you (the original and the rendered version) and your e-mail address are processed.        

4. Legal basis of the data processing

Processing of personal data is based on you consent according to Art. 6(1) point (a) GDPR.          

5. Recipients of the data and transfer to third countries (Non-EU countries)

 

No personal data is received by any other recipients than the user of our app. No data is transfered to third countries.

 

6. Duration of processing, deletion of data     

After sending the e-mail to the user, the snapshots are deleted from the local directory after 1 minute. The e-mail sent to the user (including the e-mail address) is not stored on the e-mail account, as the function "save copy of outgoing e-mails" is disabled. If the user chooses not to send the snapshots to an e-mail account the two images in the local directory will be deleted from the device after 2 minutes.
